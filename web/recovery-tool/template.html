AmberKey Recovery

Someone you care about prepared this for you. This page reconstructs the instructions and keys they left behind. It works entirely on this computer: no internet connection is needed or used, and nothing you type leaves this page. Take your time.

Step 1 — Enter the cards

Type the word phrase from each AmberKey card, one card per line. Copy the words exactly; capitalization does not matter.

    Step 2 — Load the bundle file

    The bundle is a single file, usually named like continuity-bundle.age. It may be on a USB drive that came with a card, or downloaded from AmberKey.

    Step 3 — Unlock

    The packet

    Before anything else: do not cancel the phone number of the person who died until every account is settled. Many accounts confirm changes by text message.
